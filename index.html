About Me

I have a wife and a 10 month daughter who is my world. Currently I work as the IT specialist for MasterCorp Inc at their Corporate office in Orlando and working on moving to the Developing Team.
I have been training Brazilian Jiu Jitsu for 8 years, currently I'm a brown belt and a few years away from becoming a black belt.
If I'm not spending time with my family or working I'm definitely coding or training. My Goal is to become a Senior Developer at my company.
